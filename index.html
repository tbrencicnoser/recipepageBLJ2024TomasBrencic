Cinnamon Stars

×

INGREDIENTS

3 egg whites 250g powdered sugar 1 tsp lemon juice 1 tsp cinnamon 300g ground almonds

Step 1.

Whip Egg Whites: Beat the egg whites in a clean bowl until stiff.

Step 2.

Incorporate Sugar and Cinnamon: Gradually add the powdered sugar and lemon juice while continuing to beat until the mixture is glossy and stiff.

Step 3.

Set aside about a quarter of the mixture for the glaze.

Step 4.

Add Almonds: Gently fold in the cinnamon and ground almonds into the remaining egg white mixture until a firm dough forms.

Step 5.

It should look something like this:

Step 6.

Cut Out Stars: Use a star-shaped cookie cutter to cut out stars and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Step 7.

Brush with Glaze: Brush the stars with the reserved egg white glaze.

Step 8

Bake: Bake the cinnamon stars in a preheated oven at 150째C (fan 130째C) for about 10-15 minutes. The stars should remain pale and not brown. Cool: Allow the cinnamon stars to cool on the tray before gently lifting them off.
